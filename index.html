Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

SlipScore ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use and protect your personal data.

1. Data we collect

We collect your name, phone number, employment details and income information. We verify your income through UPI transaction data accessed via bank account linking with your explicit consent.

2. How we use your data

Your data is used to generate verified payslips, report payments to credit bureaus, and match you with suitable loan products from our NBFC partners. We never sell your data to third parties.

3. Data sharing

Your data is shared with NBFC lending partners only when you apply for a loan, and only with your explicit consent. You can revoke this consent at any time from the app.

4. Data storage

All data is stored on servers in India in compliance with RBI data localisation requirements. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

5. Your rights

You have the right to access, correct or delete your personal data at any time. Contact us at hello@slipscore.in to exercise these rights.

6. Contact

For privacy queries: hello@slipscore.in

Last updated: June 2026

By using SlipScore, you agree to these terms. Please read them carefully.

1. Service description

SlipScore provides payslip generation and credit building services. We are a Loan Service Provider (LSP) operating under RBI Digital Lending Guidelines 2022. We do not disburse loans directly — loans are provided by our registered NBFC partners.

2. Subscription

Monthly subscription fees (₹200/₹500/₹1,000) are collected via UPI. Subscriptions auto-renew monthly. You may cancel at any time. Refunds are subject to our refund policy.

3. Payslip validity

SlipScore payslips are generated based on income data you provide and bank verification. They are intended for use as income documentation. Acceptance by third parties is at their discretion.

4. Credit building

Credit score improvement depends on your payment consistency and is subject to credit bureau policies. SlipScore does not guarantee a specific credit score outcome.

5. Savings return

A portion of your subscription is returned after 12 consecutive months as per your plan. This is subject to completing all 12 payments without default.
