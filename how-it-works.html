Common questions

Things people ask us

Is the payslip legally valid? +
Yes. SlipScore payslips are generated based on verified UPI bank data and issued by a registered company. They are accepted by NBFCs for loan applications, by landlords for rental agreements, and by embassies for visa applications. They carry our company stamp, registration number and a QR code for instant verification.
What if I miss a payment? +
Missing one payment pauses your CIBIL reporting for that month and delays your 12-month journey. You can resume the next month. However, 3 consecutive missed payments will close your account and your savings will be returned after deducting a small fee. We always send reminders before payment is due.
How is my money safe? +
All subscription payments are held in a dedicated escrow account — separate from SlipScore's operating funds. Your savings are never used for anything else. After 12 months, ₹1,900 (Basic) is returned directly to your bank account via UPI.
Do I need a formal employer? +
No. SlipScore is designed specifically for workers without a formal employer — gig workers, self-employed, daily wage earners, freelancers, shop owners, salon staff, delivery workers. Anyone with regular income qualifies.
Is the loan guaranteed? +
Members who complete all 12 payments with a clean record are pre-qualified for a loan from our NBFC partners. The final loan decision is made by the NBFC based on their credit policy. In our experience, members who complete the full 12 months have a very high approval rate.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. You can cancel your subscription at any time. Your savings up to the point of cancellation will be returned after deducting a small processing fee. Note that cancelling before 12 months means you will not receive the pre-approved loan offer.
