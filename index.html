The problem

You earn every month.
The bank still says "insufficient history."

Gig workers paid by UPI. Freelancers with irregular invoices. Salaried people with thin credit files and low card limits. India is full of reliable earners the credit system can't properly see.

90%+

of India's workforce earns informally or irregularly — cash, UPI, invoices — with no payslip a lender will accept.

Thin file

No or low CIBIL means "high risk" by default. Loans rejected, cards refused, limits stuck where they started.

3–5%/mo

is what informal moneylenders charge instead. The people who can least afford it pay the most.
